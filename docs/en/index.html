An ideal apartment in an excellent area.

Enjoy your holidays in an unbeatable area, close to everything you need.
Have a dream holidays in Cala d'Or in Mallorca.

Holidays

Relax and enjoy the views of the marina and the weather of Mallorca. Located in an excellent spot, only 50m from the Marina Marina of Cala d'Or, 5 minutes from the shopping area with lots of restaurants, bars, pubs, souvenirs, etc, and close to the beach ...

Coast and beach

Cala d'Or is surrounded by 7 beautiful beaches: Cala Serena, Cala Ferrera, Cala Gran, Cala Esmeralda, Cala Petita, Caló des Pou and Es Fortí. The closest to the property is Caló des Pou and is a 5-minute walk away. We recommend visiting the Natural Park of Mondragó and its beach.

Activities

In the area of the apartment, there is a great variety of sports and activities for the whole family: golf, tennis, diving, water skiing, sailing, island tour, tourist train that crosses Cala d'Or among many others.
